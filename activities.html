Activities supported by our institution

1. Virtual Learning Experience

Our institution understands the need of the students and acknowledges their quest for learning more. Hence we have established EduComp set ups in each and every class, so that every student experiences learning at its best.


2. Focusing on Co-curricular activities

At liberty high we accept the fact that each student is diiferent and that each of their indivisuality needs to be celebrated. Thus we not only promote academics but aslo take care that the students flourish in cocurriculars such as Dancing, Singing, Art and Crafts.


3. Sports Facilities

We provide a range of Sports in the campus and the required set up for it as well. We have excellent fields grounds for the students who are interested.

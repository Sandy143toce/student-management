LIBERTY HIGH SCHOOL

Established in 1993, the NBA accredited Liberty High School is a frontrunner in providing quality education. Our center of excellence is situated in a serene scenic campus in Bangalore.

Ours is a focused learning environment with a fair share of fun.
Liberty Institutions are known for discipline, which is an 'obligation to excellence' in delivering high quality education.
At Liberty, 'discipline' is synonymous with 'freedom with a purpose' and the purpose is 'overall development of all the people involved viz students and staff'.
